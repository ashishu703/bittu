AnoCab Pvt. Ltd. is a leading Indian manufacturer of high-quality electrical cables and wires, known for its innovation, reliability, and commitment to safety. Since its establishment in 2015, the company has gained recognition for its superior products, including power cables, control cables, and fire-resistant wires. AnoCab’s expertise extends to high-performance solutions like submarine cables and optical hybrid cables, reinforcing its position as a trusted industry leader. With a strong focus on quality and innovation, the company continues to expand its market presence, ensuring safe and durable wiring solutions for homes, industries, and infrastructure projects.
